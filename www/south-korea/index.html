The 2018 Winter Olympic Games are underway in the South Korean county of Pyeongchang — not be confused with Pyongyang, the capital of North Korea.

Situated in the South Korean mountains, the area's name is traditionally spelled in English as Pyongchang. To distinguish that from its nuclear-armed neighbor, the Olympic bid committee added an e and a capital C to change the name to PyeongChang.

That said, do people even know where South Korea actually is? Let's find out.
